What are Mood Disorders?

Mood disorders, such as depression and bipolar disorder, affect people emotionally and can significantly impact all areas of life. Symptoms may include constantly feeling sad or having extreme mood swings that range from being very sad, empty, or cranky to being very happy.

Mood disorders are more common in women and are highly treatable. Getting a comprehensive assessment is the first step toward managing the condition and regaining stability and wellbeing.

With proper treatment, you can achieve emotional stability and build a fulfilling life.
