What is ADHD?

Attention-deficit/hyperactivity disorder (ADHD) is a long-term condition that includes a mix of ongoing problems like having a hard time paying attention, being hyperactive, and being impulsive. It often continues into adulthood.

Children and adults with ADHD may also experience low self-esteem, troubled relationships, and poor performance in school or work. The good news: ADHD is highly manageable with proper support and treatment.

Our providers create tailored treatment plans to help you learn strategies to be successful, whether in school, work, or your personal relationships.
