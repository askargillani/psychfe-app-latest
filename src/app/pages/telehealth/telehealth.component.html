Why Choose Telehealth?

  • Convenient virtual appointments from home
  • Flexible scheduling to fit your lifestyle
  • Same quality care as in-person visits
  • Secure and confidential sessions
  • No travel time required

How to Get Started

  1. Click on your preferred provider's "Schedule Appointment" button
  2. Complete your intake information on Doxy.me
  3. Choose your preferred appointment time
  4. Join your session at the scheduled time
  5. Ensure you have a private, quiet space for your appointment

Note: Telehealth appointments are conducted via secure video conferencing. You will receive login instructions via email before your appointment. For technical support during your session, please have our office phone number ready.
