What is Medication Management?

Medication management is a level of outpatient treatment involving the initial evaluation of your need for psychotropic medications, provision of a prescription, and ongoing medical monitoring.

This comprehensive process includes thorough review of prescribed drugs and their possible side effects to create treatment plans and monitor their safety and efficacy. We monitor, reconcile medications, and ensure you achieve desired outcomes.

Our goal is to find the right medication at the right dose to optimize your mental health and quality of life.
