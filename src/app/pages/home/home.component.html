Expert Psychiatric Care for Your Mental Health Journey

Comprehensive Mental Health Treatment

At Circle Psychiatry, we understand that seeking mental health care is a significant step. Our board-certified psychiatrists and experienced psychiatric nurse practitioners specialize in evidence-based treatment for a wide range of mental health conditions. From initial psychiatric evaluation to ongoing medication management and therapy, we provide comprehensive care tailored to your unique needs.

We treat conditions including major depressive disorder, generalized anxiety disorder, panic disorder, ADHD, bipolar disorder, PTSD, insomnia and sleep disorders, eating disorders, and more. Our holistic approach considers your physical health, lifestyle, and personal goals to create an effective treatment plan.

Accessible Telehealth Psychiatry Services

Our telehealth psychiatry services make quality mental health care accessible regardless of your location. Through secure video appointments, you can connect with our experienced providers from the comfort and privacy of your home. This is particularly beneficial for individuals in rural areas, those with mobility challenges, or anyone with a busy schedule who finds it difficult to attend in-person appointments.

We accept most major insurance plans, making mental health care affordable and accessible. Our flexible scheduling includes evening and weekend appointments to accommodate your lifestyle. Whether you're seeking help for the first time or looking to continue care with a new provider, we make the process simple and stress-free.

Convenient Telehealth Mental Health Services

What is Telehealth Psychiatry?

Telehealth — sometimes called telemedicine — lets you see your healthcare provider for an appointment without going to their office. You can have a telehealth psychiatry visit online using your computer, tablet, or smartphone. Our secure platform ensures your privacy while providing the same quality care as in-person visits. Learn more about our teletherapy services.

Benefits of Telehealth:

  • See your provider from the comfort of your home, work, or anywhere convenient
  • Particularly helpful for those with limited mobility and those living in rural areas
  • Saves time — no travel, no time off work, no childcare arrangements needed
  • Get appointments faster and more easily, often at lower cost
  • Access providers who may be far from you
