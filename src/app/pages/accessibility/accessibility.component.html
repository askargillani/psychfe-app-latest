Accessibility Statement

We're committed to ensuring our website is accessible to everyone.

Our Accessibility Commitment

We are committed to providing a website that is accessible to individuals with disabilities, in conformance with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level A & AA. To this end, we regularly test our website in accordance with best practices, using automated and manual testing and with the assistance of accessibility consultant eSSENTIAL Accessibility which is supported by a diverse team of accessibility professionals including users of assistive technologies.

Accessibility Standards and Compliance

Our website strives to comply with the following standards and guidelines:

  • Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level A & AA
  • Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA) requirements
  • Section 508 of the Rehabilitation Act
  • Best practices for digital accessibility

Feedback and Accessibility Accommodations

We welcome your feedback. If you encounter any accessibility barriers or otherwise have difficulty using our website due to your disability, please contact us for assistance. We are dedicated to working with you to resolve accessibility issues and provide alternative ways to access our services.
