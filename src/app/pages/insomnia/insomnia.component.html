What is Insomnia?

Insomnia is a common sleep disorder that makes it hard to fall asleep or stay asleep, or causes a person to wake up too early and not be able to get back to sleep. This can drain energy, affect mood, health, work performance, and quality of life.

Insomnia is one of the most common sleep disorders and is highly treatable. Whether your insomnia is short-term or chronic, professional support can help you understand the underlying causes and develop strategies to improve your sleep.

With proper treatment and good sleep hygiene practices, you can restore restful, restorative sleep.
