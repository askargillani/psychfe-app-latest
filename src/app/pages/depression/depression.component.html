What is Depression?

Depression, also called major depressive disorder or clinical depression, is more than just feeling sad. It's a mood disorder that causes persistent feelings of sadness, hopelessness, and loss of interest in activities you once enjoyed. Depression affects how you think, feel, and behave—and can significantly impact your daily life, relationships, work, and physical health.

The important thing to know is this: depression is treatable, and you are not alone. With the right support and care, people with depression recover and go on to live full, meaningful lives.
