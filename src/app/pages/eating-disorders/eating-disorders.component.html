What are Eating Disorders?

Eating disorders are serious health conditions that affect both physical and mental health. The most common types are anorexia, bulimia, and binge-eating disorder. They involve problems in how a person thinks about food, eating, weight, and shape, leading to dangerous eating behaviors that can affect nutrition and harm body systems.

They are also linked with depression, anxiety, self-harm, and suicidal thoughts. With proper professional treatment combining therapy, medical care, and nutritional support, people with eating disorders can recover and develop a healthy relationship with food and their bodies.

Recovery is possible. You deserve support from providers who understand eating disorders and are committed to your healing.
